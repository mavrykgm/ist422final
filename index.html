What is an Adventure, Inc. Adventure?

Adventures are immersive games wherein the players become a character in the center of an ongoing story. Adventures are created in many genres, including sci-fi, fantasy, mystery, thriller and more. Players are provided background informaiton to understand their goal, and are allowed to use any of the resources on site in an effort to complete the task.

Many Adventures offer degrees of success. Completing the task may be the overall mission goal but mistakes may have consequences that effect their final team score.

Is this like an escape room?

Escape rooms are themed rooms which provide 1-2 hours to solve a set of puzzles with clues hidden throughout the room. While some of our Adventures are in rooms, they often expand the idea of an Escape Room to a larger scale. Not every item is a clue, and not every apparent clue is relevant to the Adventure. Adventures also have a stronger focus on story.

What is the difference between an Event and an Experience?

An Adventure Experience is 2-3 days long, and allow players to experience the full story intended for the scenario. In addition more points are available to be awarded and often include smaller goals that, if successful, provide benefits toward completing the main story goal. Adventure Events are shorter, typically a half-day, and focus on the final scene of the story.

What is the Logbook?

The Logbook provides a resource for players to keep track of clues that may be helpful to completing the Adventure.

  • Experience Events

Quick Play events are intended to last only a few hours. Players participating in Quick Play events are able to get a taste of the story by skipping directly to the final scene. Quick Play events run daily Monday - Thursday.

Experience events provide truly immersive game play with elements that can only be gained from the extended story. Teamwork is critical and a diverse set of talents often goes a long way. These games run from Friday through Sunday.
