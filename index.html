  • Quick Play Events
  • Experience Events

Quick Play events are intended to last only a few hours. Players participating in Quick Play events are able to get a taste of the story by skipping directly to the final scene. Quick Play events run daily Monday - Thursday.

Experience events provide truly immersive game play with elements that can only be gained from the extended story. Teamwork is critical and a diverse set of talents often goes a long way. These games run from Friday through Sunday.
